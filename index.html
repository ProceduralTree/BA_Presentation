The Cahn-Hilliard equation

Cahn-Hilliard Equation:

\begin{equation} \label{org1f8c474} \begin{aligned} \partial_{t}\phi(x,t) &= \nabla \cdot(M(\phi)\nabla\mu), \\ \mu &= - \varepsilon^2 \Delta\phi + W'(\phi), \end{aligned} \end{equation}
  • phase field equation for two phase flow
  • diffuse interface equation
  • gives position of phases
\( M(\phi): [-1,1] \to \mathbb{R}^+ \) Mobility coefficient
\( W(\phi): [-1,1] \to \mathbb{R}^+ \) Double well potential
\( \varepsilon > 0 \in \mathbb{R} \) Interface coefficient
\( \phi : \Omega \times (0,T) \to \mathbb{R}^d \) Phase-field variable
\( \mu : \Omega \times (0,T) \to \mathbb{R}^d \) Chemical potential variable
  • constant mobility \( M(\phi) \equiv 1 \)
  • polynomial potential \( W(\phi) = \frac{1}{4} \phi^2(1-\phi^2) \)
  • 0 Neumann boundary conditions
\begin{equation} \label{orge658747} \begin{aligned} \nabla\mu \cdot \mathbf{n} &= 0 & \text{on} \, \partial\Omega &\times (0,T),\\ \partial_n\phi &= 0 & \text{on} \, \partial\Omega &\times (0,T), \end{aligned} \end{equation}

Properties

  • Mass conservative
  • Total energy decreases
  • expensive

Baseline solver

  • Implicit in time
  • discretized on NxN grid
  • uses multi-grid scheme
  • solves equation of type
\begin{equation} DL \cdot \begin{pmatrix} \phi^{n+1}_{ij} \\ \mu^{n+\frac{1}{2}}_{ij} \end{pmatrix} = b \end{equation}

where

\( b \) collects all terms not dependant on \( \phi_{ij}^{n+1} \)
\( DL \) Jacobian of \( L \)
\( L \) Implicit terms in discretization

Relaxation

Relaxed Cahn Hilliard equation

\begin{equation} \label{orgc27d7a8} \begin{aligned} \partial_t \phi^\alpha &= \Delta \mu \,,\\ \mu &= \varepsilon ^2 \alpha(c^\alpha - \phi^\alpha) + W'(\phi) . \end{aligned} \end{equation}

Additional elliptical system

\begin{align} \label{orga2505b4} - \Delta c^\alpha + \alpha c^a &= \alpha \phi ^\alpha, \end{align}
